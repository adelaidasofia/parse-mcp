# Quarterly Engineering Review

Reliability work dominated the quarter. The team cut p95 latency by a third after replacing the legacy serializer on the hot path.

The migration to the new store completed on schedule, with zero customer-visible downtime during the cutover window.

Security hardening landed across all public endpoints, including stricter input validation and per-tenant rate limits.

Documentation received a long-overdue refresh, and the onboarding guide now reflects the current build pipeline.

Hiring slowed deliberately to protect onboarding quality, and two senior engineers joined the platform group this quarter.

Looking ahead, the roadmap prioritizes multi-region readiness and a deeper investment in automated evaluation.
