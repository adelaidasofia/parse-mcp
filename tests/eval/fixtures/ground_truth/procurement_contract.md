# Master Services Agreement Extract

## Section 4. Service Levels

The Provider shall maintain availability of no less than 99.9 percent measured monthly. Service credits accrue per the table below when availability falls beneath the committed threshold.

| Monthly Uptime | Service Credit |
| --- | --- |
| 99.0 to 99.9 percent | 5 percent |
| 95.0 to 99.0 percent | 15 percent |
| Below 95.0 percent | 30 percent |

Section 5. Indemnification. Each party shall indemnify the other against third-party claims arising from its gross negligence or willful misconduct, subject to the limitations in Section 8.
